Let $X$ be a topological space and $x\in X$. $X$ is said to be \emph{locally simply connected at} $x$, if every neighborhood  of $x$ contains a simply connected neighborhood of $x$.

$X$ is said to be locally simply connected if it is locally simply connected at every point.
