0.1 Introduction

Definition 0.1.

Let us recall that a Polish space is a separable, completely metrizable topological space, and that Polish groups GP are metrizable (topological) groups whose topology is Polish, and thus they admit a compatible metric d which is left-invariant; (a topological group GT is metrizable iff GT is Hausdorff, and the identity e of GT has a countable neighborhood basis).
