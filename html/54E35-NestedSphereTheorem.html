nested sphere theorem


In a metric space X, let B¯r⁢(x) be the closed ball centered at x∈X with radius r>0.

Theorem 1 (Nested sphere theorem [KF]).

A metric space X is complete if and only if every sequence {B¯rn⁢(xn)}n such that B¯ri+1⁢(xi+1)⊆B¯ri⁢(xi) and rn→0 when n→∞ has a nonempty intersection (i.e. ⋂n=1∞B¯rn⁢(xn)≠∅).
