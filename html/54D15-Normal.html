normal


A topological space X is said to be normal if X is T1 (i.e. singletons are closed), and for all disjoint closed sets D,F⊆X there exist disjoint open sets U and V such that C⊆U and D⊆V (i.e, X is also T4).

Some authors do not require the T1 axiom as part of this definition.
