Markov Kakutani fixed point theorem


Theorem [Markov - Kakutani] - Let X be a topological vector space and 𝒯 a commuting family of continuous linear operators in X. Suppose K is a compact convex subset of X such that

T⁢(K)⊆K

for every T∈𝒯. Then there is a point x0∈K such that T⁢x0=x0 for all T∈𝒯.
