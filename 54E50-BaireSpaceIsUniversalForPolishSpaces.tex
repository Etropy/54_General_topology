The following result says that Baire space, $\mathcal{N}$, is \emph{universal} for Polish spaces.

\begin{theorem*}
Let $X$ be a nonempty Polish space. Then, there is a continuous and onto map $f\colon\mathcal{N}\rightarrow X$.
\end{theorem*}
