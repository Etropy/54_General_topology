Let $X$ be a topological space and $Y$ a subspace of $X$. If there exists a continuous map $r:X \to Y$ such that $r(y)=y$ for all $y \in Y$, then we say $Y$ is a \emph{retract} of $X$ and $r$ is a \emph{retraction}.
